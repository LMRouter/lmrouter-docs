title: Models
description: Models & services supported by LMRouter
---

LMRouter supports a wide range of models and services, including:

* Large Language Models
* Image Models
* Audio Models
* Video Models (coming soon)
* Embedding Models
* Web Search Services (coming soon)
* Code Interpreter Services (coming soon)

A full list of models and services supported by LMRouter can be found in the [models](https://lmrouter.com/models) page.
